Project Narrative

A hilltop sanctuary above the clouds.

Perched at 1,600m in the lush hills of Munnar, Neural Sanctuary E2E is a premium boutique retreat designed for those who seek stillness without sacrificing luxury. Wake up to cascading mist rolling through the tea gardens, sip hand-picked estate tea on your private deck, and surrender to the silence of Kerala's most enchanting highlands.


The property features sustainably sourced teakwood interiors, a heated infinity pool, and a curated in-room experience crafted by our on-site concierge team.
